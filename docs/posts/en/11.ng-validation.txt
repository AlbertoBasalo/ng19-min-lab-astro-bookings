Day 1️⃣1️⃣ : Validation

✅ Add the validation attributes to the template
<input
    name="email"
    [(ngModel)]="email"
    required
    email
/>

✅ Add a reference to the ngModel and use its state
<input
    name="email"
    [(ngModel)]="email"
    #emailModel="ngModel"
    required
    email
    [attr.aria-invalid]="emailModel.invalid"
/>

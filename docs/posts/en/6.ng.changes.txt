Angular tutorial day 6 : Signals

- Add signal properties to the component
cookiesAccepted: WritableSignal<boolean> = signal<boolean>(false);

- Signals can change over time
protected acceptCookies() {
  this.cookiesAccepted.set(true);
}

- Call methods from the template  
<button (click)="acceptCookies()">Accept cookies</button>

- Templates can show or hide elements based on a condition
